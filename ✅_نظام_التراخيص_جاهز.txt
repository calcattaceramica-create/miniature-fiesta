EXAMPLE: Create New License
================================================================================

1. Type: 1 and press Enter

2. Enter Client Information:
   Client Name: Mohamed Ahmed
   Company [optional]: Success Company
   Email [optional]: mohamed@example.com
   Phone [optional]: 0123456789

3. Enter Admin Information:
   Username: mohamed
   Password: mohamed123

4. Choose License Type:
   1. Trial - 30 days
   2. Monthly - 30 days
   3. Quarterly - 90 days
   4. Semi-Annual - 180 days
   5. Yearly - 365 days
   6. Lifetime

   Choose (1-6): 5

5. Enter License Limits:
   Max Users [5]: 10
   Max Branches [1]: 2

6. Notes [optional]: VIP Client

7. License will be created and you will see:

   ================================================================================
   SUCCESS: License created successfully!
   ================================================================================

   License Key: LIC-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
   Client: Mohamed Ahmed
   Company: Success Company
   Type: yearly
   Max Users: 10
   Max Branches: 2
   Expires: 2027-01-19
   Days Remaining: 365 days

8. A file will be saved with all information:
   license_LIC_XXXX_XXXX_XXXX_XXXX.txt
